Welcome to Aditya College of Engineering, Madanapalle

Aditya College of Engineering, Madanapalle under the umbrella of Veda Educational Society was established in the year 2009 on lofty and noble ideals to impart excellent technical and value based education under the able and dynamic leadership.

About ACEM

ACEM is beautifully nestled against an array of mountains and lush greenery about 10 km from the heart of Madanapalle. The college has a wide reputation for providing outstanding teaching and learning and has many first class facilities.
